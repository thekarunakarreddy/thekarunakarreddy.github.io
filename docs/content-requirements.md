## Content Quality Guidelines

### Writing Standards:
- **Tone**: Professional but approachable
- **Grammar**: Error-free, proofread content
- **Length**: Concise but informative
- **Keywords**: Include relevant industry terms naturally

### Image Standards:
- **Quality**: High resolution, professional appearance
- **Consistency**: Consistent style and quality across all images
- **Optimization**: Compressed for web without quality loss
- **Alt Text**: Descriptive alternative text for all images

### Technical Standards:
- **Links**: All URLs must be working and current
- **Accuracy**: All information must be accurate and up-to-date
- **Formatting**: Consistent formatting across all content sections
